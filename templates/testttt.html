BlockChain

A blockchain, is a chain of one block after another. Each block holds a certain amount of information and they are connected in a chain in the order in which they were created.

Bitcoin

The concept of Bitcoin was first proposed by Satoshi Nakamoto on 1 November 2008 and was officially born on 3 January 2009. The open source software designed and released based on Satoshi Nakamoto's ideas and the P2P network on which it is built. Bitcoin is a P2P form of digital currency. Bitcoin's transaction records are open and transparent.

Ether

Ether (or ETH) is a unit of account on the Ethereum blockchain. This is different from Bitcoin, which has the same blockchain and unit of account name, Bitcoin (or BTC)
